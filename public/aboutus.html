A bit about us

Established in 2010 and situated in Northampton’s Iconic Town Centre and set amongst some of the towns’ finest stores, pubs and restaurants. Sher Locks Combs offer the finest mens hairdressing and barbers services in Northampton.
Step inside and you will find a classically styled Gentleman’s Barber shop. Dark wood cabinets complimented by marble work surfaces and antique brass fittings, Classic red Belmont barber chairs and an Edwardian chesterfield sofa complete the look to give a calm and relaxing atmosphere.
The shop is air conditioned so you can relax in comfort whilst enjoying a selection of complimentary beverages with us.
